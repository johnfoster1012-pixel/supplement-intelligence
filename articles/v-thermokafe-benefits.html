What is V-THERMOKAFE?

V-THERMOKAFE is a thermogenic coffee blend engineered to enhance metabolic rate, promote fat oxidation, and provide sustained energy through the synergistic combination of Green Coffee Bean Extract at 400mg (standardized for chlorogenic acid), Garcinia Cambogia (HCA) at 500mg for lipogenesis inhibition, and Green Tea Extract (EGCG) at 300mg for catechin-mediated thermogenesis — all delivered in a premium Arabica coffee base.

Unlike stimulant-heavy fat burners that rely solely on caffeine and sympathomimetic compounds, V-THERMOKAFE targets three distinct metabolic pathways: chlorogenic acid reduces intestinal glucose absorption and increases hepatic fat oxidation, HCA inhibits ATP-citrate lyase to reduce de novo fat synthesis, and EGCG potentiates norepinephrine-mediated thermogenesis to increase resting metabolic rate. The Arabica coffee base provides a familiar, enjoyable delivery format with additional cognitive alertness benefits.

This product is part of the Awaken Collection by Vital Health Global and is ideal for adults pursuing weight management goals, fitness enthusiasts seeking pre-workout metabolic support, and dieters looking for evidence-based thermogenic assistance.

Research-Backed Clinical Benefits

The multi-ingredient synergy in V-THERMOKAFE delivers benefits individually supported by peer-reviewed research:

  • Increased Metabolic Rate: Green Tea Extract (EGCG) has been shown in meta-analyses to increase 24-hour energy expenditure by 4–5%, equivalent to burning an additional 80–100 calories per day without changes in diet or exercise.
  • Enhanced Fat Oxidation: Chlorogenic acid from Green Coffee Bean Extract increases hepatic fat oxidation and reduces intestinal glucose absorption, shifting the body's fuel preference toward stored fat. EGCG independently enhances fat oxidation during both rest and exercise.
  • Appetite Management: Garcinia Cambogia (HCA) has demonstrated appetite-suppressing effects in clinical trials, potentially through serotonin modulation, helping to reduce caloric intake alongside increased metabolic output.
  • Sustained Energy: The Arabica coffee base provides natural caffeine for alertness, while the thermogenic ingredients provide a sustained metabolic boost that extends beyond the acute caffeine effect.

Safety Profile

V-THERMOKAFE safety summary: Contains caffeine; avoid in cardiovascular conditions; not for use under 18

  • Green Coffee Bean Extract (400mg): Contains caffeine and chlorogenic acid. Well-tolerated. May cause mild GI effects in sensitive individuals.
  • Garcinia Cambogia (500mg): Generally safe at this dose. Rare reports of headache or GI discomfort. Avoid in liver disease.
  • Green Tea Extract (300mg EGCG): Safe at this dose. High-dose EGCG (>800mg) has rare hepatotoxicity reports — V-THERMOKAFE is well within safe range.

Contraindications:

  • Contains caffeine — avoid if caffeine-sensitive or within 6 hours of bedtime
  • Not recommended for individuals with cardiovascular conditions without medical clearance
  • Not for use during pregnancy or nursing
  • Not intended for individuals under 18 years of age
  • Avoid combining with other stimulant-containing supplements

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does V-THERMOKAFE help with weight loss?

A: V-THERMOKAFE targets weight management through three mechanisms: (1) EGCG increases resting metabolic rate by 4–5%, (2) Chlorogenic acid shifts fuel utilization toward fat oxidation and reduces glucose absorption, and (3) HCA inhibits the enzyme responsible for converting carbohydrates to stored fat. Combined with the natural caffeine from Arabica coffee, this creates a multi-pathway approach to thermogenesis and fat metabolism.

Q: When should I take V-THERMOKAFE?

A: For optimal thermogenic effect, take V-THERMOKAFE in the morning or 30 minutes before exercise. The caffeine content means it should be avoided within 6 hours of bedtime. Taking it before a workout maximizes the fat oxidation benefits during exercise.

Q: Is V-THERMOKAFE safe?

A: V-THERMOKAFE contains caffeine from Arabica coffee and Green Tea Extract. It is well-tolerated by most adults but should be avoided by individuals sensitive to caffeine, those with cardiovascular conditions, pregnant or nursing women, and anyone under 18. Total caffeine content should be considered alongside other dietary caffeine sources.

Q: How much weight can I lose with V-THERMOKAFE?

A: V-THERMOKAFE is designed to support — not replace — a calorie-controlled diet and exercise program. The thermogenic effect of EGCG (80–100 extra calories/day) combined with appetite management from HCA can contribute to a modest but meaningful caloric deficit over time. Clinical trials on green tea extract show average additional weight loss of 1–2 kg over 12 weeks compared to placebo.

Q: Can I drink V-THERMOKAFE every day?

A: Yes, V-THERMOKAFE is designed for daily use. However, monitor total daily caffeine intake from all sources (coffee, tea, other supplements) and stay within the recommended limit of 400mg caffeine per day for healthy adults.

People Also Ask

What is the best thermogenic coffee?

The most effective thermogenic coffees combine multiple fat-burning pathways: catechin-mediated thermogenesis (EGCG), glucose absorption reduction (chlorogenic acid), and lipogenesis inhibition (HCA). V-THERMOKAFE provides all three in a single Arabica coffee blend.

Does green tea extract really burn fat?

Yes. Multiple meta-analyses of randomized controlled trials confirm that green tea catechins (particularly EGCG) significantly increase fat oxidation and 24-hour energy expenditure. A Cochrane review found that green tea preparations resulted in statistically significant weight loss compared to controls.

Is Garcinia Cambogia effective for weight loss?

Evidence is mixed but supportive at adequate doses. A systematic review (PMID: 21197150) found that Garcinia Cambogia (HCA) produced small but statistically significant short-term weight loss compared to placebo. Effects are most pronounced when combined with other thermogenic compounds, as in V-THERMOKAFE.

How much caffeine is in thermogenic coffee?

V-THERMOKAFE contains natural caffeine from Arabica coffee plus additional caffeine from Green Tea Extract. Total caffeine content is moderate and comparable to a strong cup of coffee. Always check the label for exact caffeine content and factor in other dietary caffeine sources.
