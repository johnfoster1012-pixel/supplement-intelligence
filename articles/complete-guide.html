How It Works: The Science

Immediate Effects (Within 30 Minutes)

Caffeine blocks adenosine receptors, reducing feelings of fatigue and increasing dopamine signaling. You'll notice improved alertness, reaction time, and motivation.

Short-Term Effects (1-4 Weeks)

Ginkgo Biloba increases cerebral blood flow, delivering more oxygen and nutrients to brain cells. Phosphatidylserine integrates into neuronal membranes, improving signal transmission.

Long-Term Effects (8-12 Weeks)

Bacopa Monnieri's bacosides upregulate BDNF (brain-derived neurotrophic factor) and modulate acetylcholine, leading to measurable improvements in memory consolidation and recall. This is where V-NEUROKAFE truly differentiates from regular coffee.
