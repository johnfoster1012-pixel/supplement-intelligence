What is V-ORGANEX?

V-ORGANEX is a comprehensive organ cleanse and detoxification formula targeting the body's primary filtration and elimination organs: the liver, kidneys, and lymphatic system. Built around three clinically validated ingredients — N-Acetyl Cysteine (NAC) at 600mg, Alpha Lipoic Acid at 300mg, and Artichoke Leaf Extract at 400mg — V-ORGANEX provides multi-pathway detoxification support through glutathione precursor delivery, universal antioxidant protection, and hepatobiliary function enhancement.

NAC is the most well-studied glutathione precursor in clinical medicine, used in hospital settings for acetaminophen overdose and as a mucolytic agent. At supplemental doses, it reliably increases intracellular glutathione levels — the liver's primary detoxification cofactor. Alpha Lipoic Acid provides both water-soluble and fat-soluble antioxidant protection while supporting mitochondrial energy production in hepatocytes. Artichoke Leaf Extract stimulates bile production and flow, facilitating the elimination of fat-soluble toxins and supporting cholesterol metabolism.

This product is part of the Detox Collection by Vital Health Global and is ideal for adults seeking liver enzyme normalization, kidney filtration support, heavy metal detoxification, and comprehensive oxidative stress reduction.

Research-Backed Clinical Benefits

The multi-ingredient synergy in V-ORGANEX delivers benefits individually supported by peer-reviewed research:

  • Liver Enzyme Normalization: NAC has been shown in clinical trials to reduce elevated ALT and AST levels in patients with non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD), while Artichoke Extract provides hepatoprotective effects through cynarin-mediated bile stimulation and silymarin-like antioxidant activity.
  • Kidney Filtration Support: NAC's antioxidant properties protect renal tubular cells from oxidative damage, and its use as a nephroprotective agent in contrast-induced nephropathy is well-documented in clinical literature.
  • Heavy Metal Detoxification: Alpha Lipoic Acid has demonstrated chelation properties for mercury, arsenic, and cadmium in both animal and human studies, while NAC-derived glutathione conjugates heavy metals for biliary and renal excretion.
  • Oxidative Stress Reduction: The combination of NAC (glutathione precursor) and ALA (universal antioxidant) provides comprehensive free radical neutralization across all cellular compartments, reducing systemic oxidative stress markers.

Safety Profile

V-ORGANEX safety summary: NAC is well-studied and safe; avoid high doses in kidney disease

  • NAC (600mg): Well-tolerated. Rare GI side effects (nausea, diarrhea). May have mild blood-thinning effects. Strong sulfur odor is normal.
  • Alpha Lipoic Acid (300mg): Generally safe. May lower blood glucose — monitor if diabetic. Rare skin rash reported.
  • Artichoke Leaf Extract (400mg): Excellent safety profile. Avoid if allergic to Asteraceae/Compositae family plants (daisies, ragweed).

Contraindications:

  • Consult healthcare provider if you have kidney disease (NAC dosing may need adjustment)
  • Monitor blood glucose if diabetic (ALA may enhance insulin sensitivity)
  • Avoid if allergic to Asteraceae family plants
  • Consult if on blood-thinning medications

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long should I take V-ORGANEX for a detox protocol?

A: Clinical studies on NAC and ALA for liver support typically run 8–12 weeks. For a structured detox protocol, 8 weeks of daily use is recommended, followed by a maintenance dose. For ongoing liver and kidney support, V-ORGANEX can be used continuously under healthcare provider guidance.

Q: Can V-ORGANEX help with fatty liver?

A: Yes. NAC at 600mg has demonstrated significant reductions in liver enzymes (ALT, AST) and hepatic steatosis markers in NAFLD patients across multiple clinical trials (PMID: 19489730). Alpha Lipoic Acid provides additional hepatoprotective effects through mitochondrial support and oxidative stress reduction in liver cells.

Q: Is V-ORGANEX safe for daily long-term use?

A: All three ingredients have well-established safety profiles for long-term use. NAC at 600mg/day has been used safely in clinical trials lasting up to 12 months. ALA at 300mg is within the well-tolerated dose range. Artichoke Extract at 400mg has no significant adverse effects reported in clinical literature. Individuals with kidney disease should consult their healthcare provider.

Q: Can I take V-ORGANEX with V-GLUTATION?

A: Yes — this is an excellent combination. NAC in V-ORGANEX provides the cysteine precursor for glutathione synthesis, while V-GLUTATION provides pre-formed reduced glutathione. Together, they support both endogenous glutathione production and direct glutathione supplementation for maximum antioxidant and detoxification capacity.

Q: Who should take V-ORGANEX?

A: V-ORGANEX is ideal for adults with elevated liver enzymes, those on medications that stress the liver, individuals exposed to environmental toxins or heavy metals, people following a structured detox protocol, and anyone seeking comprehensive organ protection and antioxidant support.

People Also Ask

What is the best supplement for liver detox?

The most effective liver detox supplements combine a glutathione precursor (NAC), a universal antioxidant (Alpha Lipoic Acid), and a hepatobiliary stimulant (Artichoke Extract). V-ORGANEX provides all three at clinical doses, targeting multiple detoxification pathways simultaneously.

Does NAC really help the liver?

Yes. NAC is the standard-of-care treatment for acetaminophen-induced liver toxicity in emergency medicine. At supplemental doses (600mg/day), it increases hepatic glutathione levels, reduces liver enzyme elevations, and has shown benefits in NAFLD patients across multiple randomized controlled trials.

How does Alpha Lipoic Acid support detoxification?

ALA functions as a universal antioxidant (active in both water and fat environments), a mitochondrial cofactor for energy production in liver cells, and a mild heavy metal chelator. It also regenerates other antioxidants including glutathione and Vitamin C, amplifying the overall detoxification capacity.

Is artichoke extract good for the liver?

Yes. Artichoke Leaf Extract contains cynarin and chlorogenic acid, which stimulate bile production and flow (choleretic effect), facilitating the elimination of fat-soluble toxins. Clinical trials have shown reductions in total cholesterol and improvements in digestive symptoms with artichoke supplementation.
