Melatonin — 1mg

Mechanism: Melatonin is an endogenous neurohormone produced by the pineal gland in response to darkness. It acts on MT1 and MT2 melatonin receptors in the suprachiasmatic nucleus of the hypothalamus to signal the onset of the biological night, lowering core body temperature, reducing alertness, and facilitating sleep initiation. At the 1mg dose used in V-ITALAY, melatonin is particularly effective at reducing sleep onset latency without disrupting the natural sleep architecture or causing next-morning grogginess associated with higher doses. Additionally, melatonin is a potent direct free-radical scavenger with significant antioxidant properties, providing secondary cellular protection during the overnight recovery window.

Clinical Evidence (PubMed ID): 17172008
