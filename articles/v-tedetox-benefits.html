Safety Profile & Side Effects

V-TEDETOX is formulated with well-characterized herbal ingredients that have been extensively reviewed for safety in the peer-reviewed literature. The combination is generally considered safe for most healthy adults when used as directed. However, several important considerations apply:

â ï¸ Important Safety Considerations

  • Bile Duct Obstruction: Dandelion Root exerts a choleretic (bile-stimulating) effect. Individuals with known bile duct obstruction, gallstones obstructing the bile duct, or any condition requiring reduced bile flow should avoid this product without medical supervision.
  • Hepatic Medications: Milk Thistle (Silymarin) can influence cytochrome P450 enzyme activity (particularly CYP3A4 and CYP2C9), potentially affecting the metabolism of certain pharmaceutical drugs. Individuals on hepatic medications, anticoagulants, or immunosuppressants should consult their physician before use.
  • Pregnancy and Breastfeeding: Insufficient safety data exists for use of high-dose botanical extracts during pregnancy or lactation. Consult a healthcare provider before use.
  • Allergic Reactions: Dandelion Root belongs to the Asteraceae family. Individuals with known allergies to ragweed, chrysanthemums, marigolds, or daisies may experience cross-reactive responses.
  • Gastrointestinal Sensitivity: At higher doses, Ginger may cause mild heartburn or GI discomfort in sensitive individuals. Starting with 1 sachet per day and monitoring tolerance is advisable.
  • Blood Sugar and Blood Pressure: Dandelion Root may have mild hypoglycemic and antihypertensive effects. Individuals on medications for diabetes or hypertension should monitor levels and consult a physician.

No severe adverse events have been documented in the literature for the ingredient doses contained in V-TEDETOX when used by healthy adults at recommended frequencies. Always discontinue use and seek medical advice if unexpected symptoms arise.
