About This Database

This database prioritizes:

  • Human clinical research — randomized trials, systematic reviews, and meta-analyses are prioritized over animal or in vitro findings
  • PMID-linked references — cited studies are linked to the biomedical literature where available
  • Conservative evidence grading — clear distinction between stronger and more preliminary evidence
  • Practical study context — doses, durations, and outcomes are summarized from published research

This database is for educational purposes only and is not medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting any supplement regimen.

Additional supplement study pages will be added over time.
