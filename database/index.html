About This Database

This database prioritizes:

  • Human clinical trials — RCTs and meta-analyses preferred over animal/in vitro studies
  • PubMed citations — Every claim linked to peer-reviewed research with PMID
  • Conservative evidence grading — Clear distinction between strong, moderate, and preliminary evidence
  • Practical dosing information — Actual doses used in successful clinical trials

This database is for educational purposes only and is not medical advice. Always consult a healthcare provider before starting any supplement regimen.

More supplement studies will be added to this database over time.
