# Berberine Clinical Studies Summary

## Overview
Berberine is among the most clinically validated natural compounds for metabolic health, with 40+ RCTs demonstrating significant effects on blood glucose, HbA1c, and lipid parameters.

## Key Studies

### Blood Glucose & HbA1c (Type 2 Diabetes)

| Study | PMID | Dose | Duration | Key Finding |
|-------|------|------|----------|-------------|
| Yin et al., 2008 | 18397984 | 500mg 3x/day | 3 months | Equivalent to metformin: HbA1c -2% vs -1.8% |
| Zhang et al., 2010 | 20854384 | 1g 3x/day | 3 months | FBG -35.6 mg/dL; HbA1c -0.9% |
| Lan et al., 2015 (meta) | 25498346 | 0.5-1.5g/day | 8-24 weeks | FBG -15.5 mg/dL; HbA1c -0.71% |

### Lipid Profile

| Study | PMID | Dose | Duration | Key Finding |
|-------|------|------|----------|-------------|
| Kong et al., 2004 | 15531914 | 500mg 2x/day | 3 months | TC -29%; LDL -25%; TG -35% |
| Cicero et al., 2019 (meta) | 30466182 | 500-1500mg/day | Variable | LDL -23.6 mg/dL; TG -38.3 mg/dL |

### PCOS & Metabolic Syndrome

| Study | PMID | Dose | Duration | Key Finding |
|-------|------|------|----------|-------------|
| Wei et al., 2012 | 22081094 | 500mg 3x/day | 3 months | Similar to metformin for insulin sensitivity |
| An et al., 2014 | 24577384 | 400mg 3x/day | 12 weeks | Improved menstrual regularity, reduced testosterone |

## Dosage Summary
- **Blood Glucose**: 500mg 2-3x/day with meals
- **Lipid Management**: 500mg 2x/day
- **PCOS**: 400-500mg 3x/day for 3+ months
- **Bioavailability**: Take with meals; consider enhanced formulations

## Safety Notes
- Well-tolerated up to 1,500mg/day
- Common: GI symptoms (10-15%): diarrhea, constipation, abdominal pain
- IMPORTANT drug interactions: CYP3A4/CYP2D6 inhibitor
- May potentiate hypoglycemic medications
- Contraindicated: pregnancy, breastfeeding

## Evidence Grade Rationale
Grade A- — Strong evidence from multiple large RCTs and meta-analyses for glucose and lipid effects; comparable to metformin in head-to-head trials.
