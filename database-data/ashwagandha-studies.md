# Ashwagandha Clinical Studies Summary

## Overview
Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera) is an adaptogenic herb with over 30 RCTs demonstrating significant effects on stress, anxiety, cortisol, testosterone, and athletic performance.

## Key Studies

### Stress & Anxiety

| Study | PMID | Dose | Duration | Key Finding |
|-------|------|------|----------|-------------|
| Chandrasekhar et al., 2012 | 23439798 | 300mg KSM-66 2x/day | 60 days | 69.7% reduction in HAM-A; 27.9% cortisol reduction |
| Lopresti et al., 2019 | 30854916 | 240mg Shoden | 60 days | Significant HAM-A/DASS-21 reduction; 23% cortisol reduction |
| Salve et al., 2019 | 32021735 | 125-600mg/day | 8 weeks | All doses reduced PSS; 600mg strongest cortisol effect |

### Testosterone & Male Health

| Study | PMID | Dose | Duration | Key Finding |
|-------|------|------|----------|-------------|
| Wankhede et al., 2015 | 26609282 | 300mg KSM-66 2x/day | 8 weeks | 15.3% testosterone increase vs 2.7% placebo |
| Ahmad et al., 2010 | 19501822 | 5g root powder/day | 3 months | 167% improvement in sperm count |

### Athletic Performance

| Study | PMID | Dose | Duration | Key Finding |
|-------|------|------|----------|-------------|
| Shenoy et al., 2012 | 23125505 | 500mg 2x/day | 8 weeks | 13% VO2 max increase vs 2.8% placebo |
| Ziegenfuss et al., 2018 | 29520932 | 300mg Sensoril 2x/day | 12 weeks | Enhanced recovery, velocity, power |

### Sleep Quality

| Study | PMID | Dose | Duration | Key Finding |
|-------|------|------|----------|-------------|
| Langade et al., 2019 | 31728244 | 300mg KSM-66 2x/day | 10 weeks | 72% improvement in sleep quality |

## Dosage Summary
- **Stress/Anxiety**: 300mg KSM-66 2x/day or 240mg Shoden
- **Sleep**: 300mg KSM-66 2x/day
- **Testosterone**: 300mg KSM-66 2x/day for 8+ weeks
- **Athletic Performance**: 300-600mg/day standardized extract

## Safety Notes
- Generally well-tolerated up to 1,250mg/day
- Common: mild GI upset, drowsiness
- Caution: hyperthyroidism, pregnancy, autoimmune conditions
- Rare liver injury case reports documented (2021-2023)

## Evidence Grade Rationale
Grade B+ — Multiple high-quality RCTs for stress/anxiety; testosterone and performance evidence moderate but consistent.
