Quick answer

Glutathione is the body's primary intracellular antioxidant. The strongest commercial opportunity on your site is to treat it as an ingredient entity first and a product feature second.

V-GLUTATION PLUS combines 500mg of reduced glutathione with 250mg of Vitamin C and 100mg of Alpha Lipoic Acid to deliver a synergistic, multi-layered antioxidant defense system. Clinical research demonstrates that this combination supports cellular detoxification, immune function, and oxidative stress reduction -- with Vitamin C and Alpha Lipoic Acid actively regenerating and recycling glutathione to extend its biological activity. Mechanism

Glutathione neutralizes reactive oxygen species directly and supports phase II detoxification. Vitamin C and alpha lipoic acid can help recycle oxidized glutathione back into its active reduced form.

Dosage context

Human studies cited in your repository support 500 mg/day as a practical oral dose for raising blood glutathione, with some liver-health data at 300 mg/day.
