## Mechanism of Action

The mechanism is: Caffeine blocks adenosine A1/A2A receptors to acutely elevate dopamine, norepinephrine, and acetylcholine availability, while Bacopa Monnieri's active bacosides enhance synaptic signal transmission by modulating acetylcholinesterase activity and upregulating BDNF expression, supporting hippocampal neuroplasticity. Phosphatidylserine integrates into neuronal phospholipid bilayers to maintain membrane fluidity and voltage-gated ion channel function, facilitating more efficient neurotransmitter release and receptor binding. Ginkgo Biloba's ginkgolides inhibit platelet-activating factor (PAF), reduce oxidative stress via flavonoid free-radical scavenging, and increase cerebral blood flow, collectively amplifying nutrient and oxygen delivery to neurons and synergizing with the upstream nootropic actions of the other three ingredients.
