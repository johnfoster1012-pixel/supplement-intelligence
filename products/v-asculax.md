## Product Overview

V-ASCULAX combines horse chestnut extract, diosmin, hesperidin, and ginkgo biloba to support blood vessel wall integrity, reduce venous insufficiency, improve peripheral circulation, and support cardiovascular function.

**Ideal for:** Adults with varicose veins, poor peripheral circulation, leg swelling, or cardiovascular health concerns
