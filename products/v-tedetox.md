## Evidence Grade

Based on 12 peer-reviewed studies, the evidence grade for Senna Leaf (the primary active ingredient) is Grade B.

Senna has strong mechanistic and clinical evidence including multiple RCTs demonstrating reliable laxative efficacy, but its grade is capped at B rather than A due to relatively short trial durations, heterogeneous patient populations across studies, and the fact that most trials evaluate sennoside preparations rather than whole-leaf extracts at the exact dose present in this product.
