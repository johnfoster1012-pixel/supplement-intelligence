## What Does The Research Say?

Whey protein isolate is among the most thoroughly studied dietary proteins for promoting skeletal muscle anabolism. Clinical randomized controlled trials consistently show that whey protein supplementation accelerates gains in lean mass and strength when combined with resistance training. Volek et al. ## Mechanism of Action

The mechanism is: Whey protein isolate provides a rapidly absorbed bolus of essential amino acids â particularly leucine â that activates the mammalian target of rapamycin complex 1 (mTORC1) signaling pathway in skeletal muscle cells, stimulating ribosomal protein S6 kinase 1 (S6K1) and eukaryotic initiation factor 4E-binding protein 1 (4E-BP1) phosphorylation, which collectively upregulate translational machinery and accelerate muscle protein synthesis. The BCAA fraction, especially L-leucine, acts as a direct nutrient sensor and allosteric activator of mTORC1 via the Ragulator-Rag GTPase complex on the lysosomal membrane, driving amino acid uptake and anabolic gene expression independent of insulin signaling. L-Isoleucine additionally stimulates glucose uptake in muscle cells via GLUT4 translocation, while L-Valine contributes to nitrogen balance and serves as an oxidative substrate during prolonged exercise.
